Huai-Hsuan "Sandy" Chiu

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Astrophysics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Prof. Mateusz Ruszkowski. Prior to that, I earned my B.S. in Physics from National Taiwan University in 2022.

My research centers on the physics of cosmic rays and their role in galaxy evolution. I use high-resolution simulations to model cosmic ray transport, synchrotron emission, and feedback processes in various astrophysical environments, bridging theoretical predictions with multi-wavelength observations.
